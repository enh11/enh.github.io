About me

I'm a young researcher at the Center for Information Technologies and Applied Mathematics at the University of Nova Gorica, Slovenia. My research focuses on algebra, particularly hypercompositional algebra, with an emphasis on its implementation in the Rust programming language. I'm also interested in the algebraic applications of cryptography.
